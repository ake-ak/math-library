Course Structure

Each course is organized as follows:

  • Introduction: Overview, prerequisites, and learning objectives.
  • Modules: Organized topical units, each covering specific concepts.
  • Lessons: Detailed explanations with examples and worked problems.
  • References: Additional resources, books, and related topics.
  • Status: Indicates whether a course is Active, Upcoming, or Archived.

How to Choose a Course

If you are not sure which course to take, consider starting with the Mathematics learning path, which provides guidance on how different courses relate to each other.

You may also discuss course recommendations with our community, or contribute your own course materials.
