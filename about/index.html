About Math Library

Our mission, principles, and commitment to mathematical knowledge.

Our Mission

Math Library exists to preserve and make accessible the timeless knowledge of mathematics. We believe that mathematical thinking is a human birthright—essential to understanding the natural world, solving problems, and engaging with ideas that transcend time and culture.

Our mission is to create a knowledge commons where:

  • Mathematical concepts are explained with clarity and rigor
  • Learning resources remain free and accessible to all
  • The architecture of knowledge is transparent and discoverable
  • Content is preserved for long-term value rather than commercial cycles

Core Values

Accessibility

Knowledge should not be gatekept behind paywalls or obscured by jargon. We are committed to making mathematics accessible to anyone with curiosity and dedication.

Rigor

We value mathematical truth and precision. Our resources are carefully constructed to be both accessible and accurate, building understanding from clear first principles.

Preservation

Mathematics is timeless. We design our content and systems for long-term preservation, avoiding dependencies on trendy technologies that become obsolete.

Community

Learning is a communal activity. We foster spaces for questions, discussion, and collaboration among mathematicians and learners at all levels.

Clarity

Good mathematics should be clear. We prioritize semantic structure, readability, and pedagogical effectiveness in all our materials.

Our Approach

Math Library differs from many educational platforms in deliberate ways:

Content Over Presentation

We prioritize the quality and accuracy of content over flashy design. Our aesthetic is clean, semantic, and designed for readability—not distraction.

Depth Over Breadth

Rather than offering superficial coverage of everything, we focus on developing deep understanding of fundamental mathematical concepts.

Preservation Over Trends

We use technologies and approaches designed to outlast current fashions. Your learning materials will remain accessible and readable decades from now.

Open Over Commercial

Math Library is not driven by profit motives or algorithmic engagement metrics. We are committed to serving the learning community authentically.

Community Contributors

Math Library is built and sustained by mathematicians, educators, and learners who believe in the mission of accessible mathematical knowledge. We welcome contributions from the community in the form of:

  • Improvements to existing materials
  • New learning resources and courses
  • Discussions and clarifications
  • Feedback and suggestions

If you're interested in contributing, visit our Contribute page.
